Color Scheme for Chamber of Commerce Project

The color scheme the Chillicothe Mound Chamber of Commerce will have is the following:

Brunswick Green Cream Harvest Gold Outer Space Crayola
Brunswick Green (#258238) will be used primarily as a background color for the whole page. It will also be used to highlight headings and navigation. Cream (#F3FFC6) will mainly be used as a background color for text. Harvest Gold (#D8973C) will be used to separate sections and as boarders. Outer Space Crayola (#34403A) will be used for any shadow effect on the page and some text.
