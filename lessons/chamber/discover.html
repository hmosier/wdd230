Discover Chillicothe Mound

Historic Downtown

Chillicothe Mound's historic downtown features buildings that are still in use from the late 1700s.

Yoctangee Park

Chillicothe Mound features beautiful parks that wind along the Scioto River.

Historic Architecture

Because Chillicothe Mound has been around for so many years, many of its downtown buildings feature historic arcitecture, such as the courthouse.

Support Local Businesses

Some of the greatest things about living and working in Chillicothe Mound is the nature of business here. Because it is such a rural community, small businesses thrive.

Chillicothe Mound Chamber of Commerce Supports Businesses

The greatest part about our chapter of the Chamber of Commerce is how we support you and get you and the community involved with each other.
