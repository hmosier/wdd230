Site Purpose:

The Temple Inn and Suites Website is to provide information about the Temple Inn and Suites hotel chain, headquartered in Bethesda, MD. This includes information about specialized services they offer to meet needs of temple workers, patrons and their families, as well as anyone who travels to participate in events such as sealings, weddings, receptions and youth trips. The company builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near temples of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints throughout the world, and needs a centralized place to be able to provide information about their company and a way for their customers to make reservations.

Target Audience:

The target audience for the Temple Inn and Suites website is to provide lodging information and accomodations to those who are seeking to travel for any number of reason to a temple of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, including, but not limited to: weddings, sealings, receptions and youth trips. The target audience also includes those who are temple workers and missionaries who are seeking longer-term accomidations.

Color Scheme

The color scheme the Temple Inn and Suites website will have is as follows:

Maximum Blue Purple Eggshell Deep Space Sparkle Dark Purple
Maximum Blue Purple (#9CA3DB) will be used primarily as a background color for the whole page. It will also be used as a hover highlight on the navigation. Eggshell (#E6E1C5) will mainly be used as a background color for text. Deep Space Sparkle (#395C6B) will be used for heading backgrounds. Dark Purple (#28112B) will be used for shadow effects and borders, as well as main typography.
