Reservations

In order to keep things as simple as possible, we only offer one style of room: a suite. It has a full kitchen, living area, two queen beds and two sleeper sofas. It sleeps up to 8. All of our rooms are also ADA accessible.

Suites can be grouped together for extended family and weddings. For more information on this service, please visit ourreceptionspage orcontact us.

Guest Information

Deposit:

All of our rooms have a $25 deposit, plus 10% of the total room cost.

Booking/Cancellation Policy:

If you cancel your booking more than 48 hours prior to your date of arrival, you will receive a full refund, minus a $25 cancellation fee.

Cancellations less than 24 hours prior to your date of arrival will incur a $25 cancellation fee plus the cost of the first night booked.
