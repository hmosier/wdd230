Reservations

In order to keep things as simple as possible, we only offer one style of room: a suite. It has a full kitchen, living area, two queen beds and two sleeper sofas. It sleeps up to 8. All of our rooms are also ADA accessible.

Suites can be grouped together for extended family and weddings. For more information on this service, please visit our receptions page or contact us.

Guest Information

Deposit:

All of our rooms have a 50% deposit of the total room cost. The rest of the reservation fee will be charged upon arrival. Should you incur any other expenses during your stay, they will be charged upon check-out.

Booking/Cancellation Policy:

If you cancel your booking more than 24 hours prior to your date of arrival, you will receive a full refund, minus a $25 cancellation fee.

Cancellations less than 24 hours prior to your date of arrival will incur a $25 cancellation fee plus the cost of the first night booked.

Please read the following before submitting your request

  • Photo ID and Credit Card used at time of booking must be presented at time of check-in
  • Deposit amound will be charged to your credit card at time of booking
  • The rest of your room fee will be charged upon arrival
  • Check in anytime after 2pm. Specialized services are available to temple patrons earlier in the day if needed. Please call us to inquire about these accommodations.
