Full-time Missionary Accomodations

In order to accommodate full-time missionaries who may be called to serve in temples our properties are located close to, we offer specialized rates. These rates depend on length of stay, and can only be made bycontacting us.

We do our best to try to make our rates affordable, while offering comfort and convience while you serve the Lord. In addition to all of our regular stay services and ammenities, missionaries will have access to:

  • An available shuttle to/from grocery stores from 9 am to 5 pm Monday through Saturday.
  • An available shuttle to/from doctor visits.
  • Monday night FHE activities hosted by our receptions department.
  • Dinner provided every Sunday and Monday nights.
